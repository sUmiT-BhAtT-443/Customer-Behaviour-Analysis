# 📊 Power BI Dashboard

The project includes two interactive Power BI dashboard pages designed to analyze customer purchasing behavior, revenue trends, customer demographics, product performance, and business opportunities. Interactive slicers, KPI cards, and bookmark navigation allow users to explore insights dynamically.

---

# 📈 Dashboard 1: Customer Revenue Analysis

<p align="center">
  <img src="https://i.postimg.cc/2Ss2rjZ2/Customer-Revenue-Analysis.png" alt="Customer Revenue Analysis Dashboard" width="1000">
</p>

### 🎯 Dashboard Objective
Analyze customer purchasing patterns, revenue distribution, spending behavior, and demographic trends to identify key revenue drivers.

### 📌 KPIs
- Total Customers
- Unique Products
- Average Review Rating
- Total Revenue
- Average Customer Spend

### 📊 Analysis Performed
- Revenue by Gender
- Revenue by Category
- Revenue by Shipping Type
- Revenue by Season
- Revenue by Payment Method
- Revenue by Age Distribution
- Customer Count & Average Revenue by Location

### 💡 Key Insights
- Electronics generate the highest revenue among all categories.
- Male customers contribute the largest share of total revenue.
- Customers aged 51+ generate the highest revenue contribution.
- Standard and Express shipping methods have the highest order volumes.
- New York, Los Angeles, and Chicago are among the top revenue-generating locations.
- Debit Card, Cash, and Credit Card are the most preferred payment methods.

### 🚀 Business Impact
- Supports customer segmentation strategies.
- Helps identify high-value customer groups.
- Improves category-level decision making.
- Enables revenue optimization through data-driven insights.

# 📉 Dashboard 2: Best vs Worst Analysis

<p align="center">
  <img src="https://i.postimg.cc/dtX9jQnW/Best-Worst.png" alt="Best vs Worst Analysis Dashboard" width="1000">
</p>

### 🎯 Dashboard Objective
Identify the highest and lowest performing products and locations based on ratings and revenue contribution.

### 📊 Top Performance Analysis
- Top 5 Products by Rating
- Top 5 Products by Revenue
- Top 5 Revenue Generating Locations

### 📊 Bottom Performance Analysis
- Bottom 5 Products by Rating
- Bottom 5 Products by Revenue
- Bottom 5 Revenue Generating Locations

### 💡 Key Insights
- Belt, Jewelry, Pants, and Blouse are among the highest-rated products.
- Phone, Bag, and Watch generate the highest revenue.
- New York, Houston, and Los Angeles are the best-performing locations.
- Headphones and Watch receive comparatively lower customer ratings.
- Kansas, Rhode Island, and Arizona contribute the lowest revenue among locations.
- Several low-revenue products present opportunities for inventory and marketing optimization.

### 🚀 Business Impact
- Identifies best-selling products for promotion.
- Highlights underperforming products requiring improvement.
- Supports inventory planning and stock optimization.
- Improves marketing and pricing strategies.
- Enables data-driven business decision making.
